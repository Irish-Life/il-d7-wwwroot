Click here for a Sales Idea for non-married couples Non married couples – 1 in 10 people are co-habiting in Ireland. Are any of your clients in this group? Most people are aware that benefits passing between married couples are exempt from Inheritance & Gift Tax. However this only applies in the case of “Legal Spouses” and registered Civil Partners. All other couples are treated as strangers for capital acquisitions tax purposes.
