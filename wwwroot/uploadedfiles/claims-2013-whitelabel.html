Claims Statistics for 2013 covering death and illness claims

This map shows the amount of Specified Illness Cover (SIC) and Death Claims paid out by Irish Life and Canada Life in 2013. The info is broken down by county.

 
 

Specified Illness Claims (SIC)

  • In 2013, Irish Life and Canada Life paid out over €55.5 million in specified illness cover.
  • The main causes of claims were malignant cancer 58%, and heart-related conditions 22%.

Death Claims

  • In 2013, Irish Life and Canada Life paid out more than €111.9 million.
  • The average claim payment was €65,560 per claim.
  • The average age of adult claims was 67.
  • Cancer was the biggest cause of claims, accounting for 44%.
