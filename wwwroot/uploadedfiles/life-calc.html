What is life insurance?

Life insurance provides financial protection to your dependents (Typically your spouse/partner and children) if you die.

Life insurance plans are usually taken out for a fixed term, e.g. 20 years, and pay out a lump sum (The Sum Assured) to your dependents if you die before the end of term.

Nothing is paid if you survive till the end of term.


what does this calculator do?

The purpose of this calculator is to help you decide what level of life insurance you should have and how long you should have insurance for.


How does the calculator work?

You enter the income that you think your dependents would need if you died and you also decide how many years the income will be needed for.

The calculator estimates the lump sum that would be needed today to generate the chosen income for the specified number of years.

We then add on any once-off expenses that you identify to arrive at an overall life insurance total


The scope of this calculator

We have tried to get the right balance between complexity and usability. You may need more or less insurance depending on the circumstance in your individual home.

We have purposely omitted any mention of social welfare payments, pension scheme benefits, existing life insurance, existing assets etc.

We have also assumed that you have separate Life insurance that will pay off your mortgage(s) in the event of your death.

Cover any costs at death such as funeral expenses, uninsured debt etc.
